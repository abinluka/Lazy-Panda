Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a session with Lazy Panda Photography?
Simply fill out our inquiry form above, call us, or reach us on WhatsApp. We'll schedule a consultation call to discuss your vision, answer all questions, and confirm availability. A 30% advance secures your booking.
How long does photo editing and delivery take?
Typically 5–10 business days for standard packages, and 3–5 days for premium packages. We deliver via a private online gallery link. Printed albums take an additional 15 days after gallery approval.
Do you travel outside Thiruvananthapuram?
Absolutely! We regularly travel across Kerala, Tamil Nadu, and beyond. For destination shoots and outstation weddings, travel and accommodation costs are added to the package. We've photographed in Rajasthan, Goa, and even abroad.
What is your cancellation and rescheduling policy?
Cancellations 60+ days before the event receive a full refund minus the booking fee. Within 60 days, 50% of the advance is retained. Rescheduling is accommodated once at no charge, subject to availability.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers (NEFT/IMPS/RTGS), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe), and cheques. A 30% advance is required at booking, 40% one week before the event, and the remaining 30% on the day.
In what formats are photos delivered?
All edited photographs are delivered in high-resolution JPEG format via a private gallery (printable resolution). RAW files are retained for 6 months. Video films are delivered in MP4 format (4K or 1080p depending on package).
