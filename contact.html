Getting Here

Finding the Studio

Well-connected and easy to reach — here's how to get to us from key landmarks.

Trivandrum International Airport

~12 km from the studio. Approximately 25–35 minutes by cab. Pre-booking recommended on peak days.

Thiruvananthapuram Central Railway Station

~5 km from the studio. 15–20 minutes by auto-rickshaw or cab. Frequent services from all major cities.

KSRTC Central Bus Station

~6 km from the studio. Auto or cab recommended. City bus Route 6 stops near Kowdiar Junction.
