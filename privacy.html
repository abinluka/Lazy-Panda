Section 01

Introduction

Lazy Panda Photography ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website, contact us, or engage our services.

By using our website or services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. We encourage you to read this document carefully. If you have any questions, please contact us at hello@lazypandaphoto.com.

This policy applies to all data collected through our website (lazypandaphoto.com), booking forms, email communications, WhatsApp interactions and any other means by which you contact or engage with us.

Section 02

Data We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us and information automatically collected when you visit our website.

Information You Provide:

  • Name, email address and phone number submitted via contact or booking forms
  • Event details including dates, locations, and service requirements
  • Budget and package preferences
  • Messages and correspondence sent to us via any channel
  • Newsletter subscription email addresses
  • Payment information (processed securely by third-party payment providers — we do not store card details)

Automatically Collected Information:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referring website and search terms
  • Device type and operating system
Section 03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to enquiries and communicate about bookings
  • To provide, manage and deliver our photography services
  • To send booking confirmations, contracts and invoices
  • To deliver your photographs and videos via our gallery system
  • To send our newsletter (only to subscribers who have opted in)
  • To improve our website, services and client experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

We will never sell your personal data to third parties, and we will never use it for any purpose other than those listed above without your explicit consent.

Section 04

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade or transfer your personal information to third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted platforms such as gallery delivery services, cloud storage, accounting software and email providers — who are contractually obligated to protect your data
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order or governmental authority
  • Business transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition or sale of assets, your data may be transferred — you will be notified in advance
Section 05

Photography Rights & Image Use

Photographs and videos we create during your session may be used by Lazy Panda Photography for the following purposes, unless you explicitly opt out in writing:

  • Portfolio display on our website and social media
  • Marketing materials including brochures and advertisements
  • Award submissions and industry competitions
  • Editorial features in photography publications

We will never sell your images to stock photography agencies or third-party commercial entities. You may request that your images not be used in our marketing by notifying us in writing before or after your session. This opt-out does not affect the quality or delivery of your final images.

Your delivered images are for personal use. Commercial use of delivered photographs requires a separate licensing agreement. Please contact us for commercial licensing enquiries.

Section 06

Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your browsing experience. We use the following types:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (anonymised data)
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

Section 07

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. These include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all website data transmission
  • Secure password-protected client gallery delivery
  • Limited staff access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security reviews of our systems
  • Secure cloud backup of all client files

While we take every reasonable precaution, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you become aware of any security breach, please contact us immediately.

Section 08

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service provider
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for marketing purposes
  • Withdrawal of consent: Withdraw consent for newsletter subscription at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@lazypandaphoto.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Contact Us
