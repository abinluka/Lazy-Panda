Pricing FAQ

Is GST included in the prices?
All listed prices are exclusive of GST (18%). GST will be added to the final invoice as per government regulations. A detailed quote with all taxes will be provided before booking confirmation.
What is the payment schedule?
30% advance to secure your booking, 40% one week before the event date, and the remaining 30% on the day of the shoot (before we begin). This applies to all packages. Custom payment plans available for large bookings.
Are travel costs included?
Local travel within 50km of Thiruvananthapuram is included. For outstation bookings, travel, accommodation and daily allowance are billed at actuals. We'll provide a complete travel cost estimate upfront with zero surprises.
Can I upgrade my package after booking?
Yes! You can upgrade to a higher tier or add on services at any time before the event, subject to availability. Downgrades are possible up to 60 days before the event date.
Do prices vary by season or peak dates?
Peak season (October–February) and auspicious wedding dates may carry a modest premium of 10–15%. We recommend booking 6–12 months in advance for the best rates and guaranteed availability.
