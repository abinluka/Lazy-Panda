Common Questions

Before You Book

How much advance is needed to secure my date?
A 30% advance payment secures your booking. For peak-season weddings we recommend booking 6–12 months ahead. Once the advance is received, your date is locked and removed from our availability calendar.
Can I book a consultation without committing to a package?
Absolutely. Our initial consultation call is completely free and carries no obligation. We encourage you to speak with us, review our work, and take your time before deciding. We'd rather earn your trust than rush a booking.
What if I don't know which package is right for me?
That's what the consultation call is for! Tell us your vision, budget and requirements — we'll recommend the package that makes the most sense. We can also build a completely custom package if needed.
Is the booking form confidential?
Yes — all enquiry information is treated with complete confidentiality and is used solely to understand your requirements and respond to your enquiry. We never share or sell personal data.
How quickly will you respond to my enquiry?
We aim to respond to every enquiry within 24 hours on business days (Monday–Saturday). For urgent enquiries, WhatsApp is the fastest route — we typically respond within a few hours.
