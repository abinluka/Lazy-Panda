What Drives Us

Our Creative Philosophy

Every great image is born from a belief — and these are ours.

01

Light Is the Language

We don't chase light — we listen to it. Every shoot begins before the camera is raised, with reading the quality, direction and emotion of available light. Whether golden-hour warmth or dramatic studio contrast, we sculpt with light as our primary medium.

02

Emotions First, Techniques Second

Technical mastery is merely the foundation. What elevates a photograph from good to unforgettable is the authentic human emotion within the frame. We invest time in knowing our subjects, earning their trust, and capturing who they truly are.

03

Storytelling Through Sequence

A single photograph captures a moment; a series captures a story. We approach every assignment with a narrative arc in mind — a beginning, middle, and emotional conclusion that together form a visual story your children will share with their children.

04

Less Editing, More Truth

Post-processing should enhance, not replace, reality. Our editing philosophy preserves skin tones, genuine expressions and the true colours of your day — layering only the cinematic feel that makes our work distinctive, never distorting what actually happened.

"We don't just take pictures — we craft visual legacies that outlive the moment."

Our studio was built on a simple conviction: that every person, every couple, every family deserves images that will move them — not just now, but twenty years from now when they gather around a table and remember what it felt like to be exactly there.

We bring this conviction to every booking, whether it's a grand wedding with 800 guests or an intimate portrait session for one. The commitment is the same. The care is identical. The result is always something extraordinary.

Meet the Founder

Arjun Mehta

Founder & Lead Photographer

Born in Thiruvananthapuram with a filmmaker's eye and a poet's soul, Arjun Mehta picked up his first camera at sixteen and never put it down. After a Fine Arts degree from NID Ahmedabad and an apprenticeship under renowned photojournalist Rajan Pillai, he returned to Kerala with a vision — to bring world-class visual storytelling to the intimate moments that matter most.

Over ten years and a thousand projects later, Arjun's work has been featured in Vogue India, Harper's Bazaar Bride, and The Hindu Bridal. His cinematic, emotionally rich style has made Lazy Panda Photography one of South India's most sought-after studios.

When not behind a camera, Arjun mentors emerging photographers, teaches weekend masterclasses, and occasionally disappears into the Western Ghats with a film camera.

A Decade of Stories

Our Journey

2015
The Studio Is Born

Arjun Mehta launches Lazy Panda Photography from a modest studio in Kowdiar, Thiruvananthapuram — with one camera, one light stand, and an unshakeable vision.

2017
First National Recognition

Wins the India Photography Award for Outstanding Wedding Photography. Work featured in Harper's Bazaar Bride. Client enquiries triple overnight.

2019
Fashion & Commercial Expansion

Opens a dedicated professional studio space with full lighting infrastructure. Launches fashion and commercial photography verticals; onboards first two team members.

2021
Going Digital — Stories Beyond Kerala

Destination weddings in Rajasthan, Goa and Sri Lanka. Pivot to short-form cinematic content. YouTube channel crosses 50,000 subscribers.

2022
Drone & Cinematic Division

Launches aerial photography and full cinematic videography service. DGCA-certified drone pilot joins the team. First feature-length wedding documentary released.

2025
1,000 Stories — and Counting

Crosses 1,000 completed projects. Wins Best Wedding Studio Kerala for the third consecutive year. The story continues — one frame at a time.
