Common Questions

Services FAQ

How far in advance should I book?
For weddings, we recommend booking 6–12 months in advance, especially for peak season (October–February). Portrait sessions can often be booked 2–4 weeks ahead. Contact us for last-minute availability.
Can I customise a package to suit my needs?
Absolutely. Every booking is unique. We're happy to mix and match services, adjust coverage hours, add videography or drone, and build a completely bespoke package that fits your exact requirements and budget.
Do you provide raw/unedited photos?
We deliver only professionally edited, colour-graded final images. Raw files are part of our creative workflow and are not included in deliveries. All edits are done in-house by our dedicated post-processing team.
What happens if it rains during an outdoor shoot?
We always have a backup plan. For outdoor shoots we monitor weather and communicate with clients 48 hours ahead. Light rain is often beautiful on camera — we've created some of our best work in monsoon conditions. If needed, we reschedule at no extra charge.
Do you offer engagement or family sessions separately?
Yes! Our portrait and family sessions are available independently from wedding packages. These are available in studio, at outdoor locations, or at your home. Baby & newborn sessions are also a beloved speciality.
